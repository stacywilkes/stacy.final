About

We understand that no one program or template is the answer to every HR challenge.

For this reason, Rogers HR uses a relational approach to designing custom HR solutions which use confidentiality and trust as its foundation. We spend extra time getting to know details of your business, strategically plan alongside management, and continue to support you with future HR needs.

Recruiting and Onboarding

We learn about your business goals and culture to align to the top accountabilities and skills of the position - and we go out to the market to find those specific people! We do this through trained experts who not only find the skills you need but also the “Right Fit” personality to drive your important goals!

We can help you fill one role – or manage your complete Recruiting process! We can even come onsite to Onboard your new hires so that they can adapt quickly to your company, policies, and position roles!
